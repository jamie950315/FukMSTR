## Interpretation

V218 closes a stale-readiness-summary loophole. A copied, old, or manually edited V204 summary can no longer satisfy launch preflight unless it proves it was generated by the same source commit that is launching and that source files were clean when the summary was generated.

This does not create trades, tune thresholds, or claim new profitability. Real-money use remains blocked until current forward monitoring, execution validation, provenance evidence, and source provenance are all clean.
